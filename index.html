Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Notary frequently asked questions.

Can you provide power of attorney document?

We do not provide any documents, we can print the document you already have prepared by an attorney or self prepared.

What does a notary do?

We validate the identity of the person signing the document.

When should I sign the document?

The document that needs to be notarized must be signed in presence of the notary to verify the authenticity of the signature.

What documents do I need to bring?

Any government issued photo ID can work, eg: Valid drivers license from any state, Military ID, Passport and State issued ID.

I dont have an ID how can my identity be verified?

If you dont have a valid government issued ID, then 2 witnesses who can vouch for your identity are required.
